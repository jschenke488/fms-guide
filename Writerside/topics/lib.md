<snippet id="safety">
    <warning>**SAFETY FIRST!**<br>When there are robots connected, always have a computer with the "Match Play" page open and the "scoring" e-stop nearby. In case of an emergency, you can disable ALL robots using the scoring e-stop button. The driver station software cannot disable robots. You must use the e-stop button.</warning>
</snippet>

<snippet id="safety-lshs">
    <warning>**SAFETY FIRST!**<br>Robots can be dangerous! In case of an emergency, you can disable the robot by pressing the SPACE BAR on the driver station laptop. To disable normally, press Enter or click the disable button.</warning>
</snippet>
